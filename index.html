Welcome to the demo of our interactive search task (Experiment 2 in the manuscript).

For convenience, we have labeled each block according to the target-scene congruence as defined in the manuscript.

You will be prompted to select a block based on the congruence level. At the end of the block, you will be prompted to select the next block.
